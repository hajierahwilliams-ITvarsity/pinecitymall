ROCOMAMAS

Delicious and affordable burgers that you just can't get enough. Cape Town is packed with burger joints that all deliver their own unique tastes and food styles. But there's nothing out there quite like a good Smashburger. Get creative with a 'build your own' Smashburger, or enjoy their selection of menu items, including ribs, wings and other amazing treats. Pair a gourmet milkshake with your preferred meal, and make sure to top it off with loaded fries. Visit our brand new RocoMamas restaurant, right here at Pine City Mall, and dive into juicy burgers as you've never tasted before.
