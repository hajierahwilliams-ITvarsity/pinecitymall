MR PRICE

Mr Price Pine City Mall is an iconic fashion store among all ages. This brand is a nationwide favourite, catering for men, women and children. You will find anything from casual clothing to formal wear, intimate items and comfortable clothes for little ones. Their in-house brands include Red, Body Material, Maxed and Oakridge. Mr Price is all about affordable fashion for everyone. Located on the upper-level corner store.
