PICK N PAY

Pick n Pay is one of South Africa's oldest and most beloved grocery stores. PnP Pine City Mall offers outstanding specials on a regular basis, making their fresh produce, essential groceries and other goods highly affordable and good value for money. If you are looking for fresh ingredients for a special meal or a quick and tasty snack for lunch, Pick n Pay is the perfect option for you. As an established brand, they strive to maintain their excellent quality standards, every time. You can find them on the lower-level in Pine city mall.
