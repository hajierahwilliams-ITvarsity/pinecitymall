iSTORE

The iStore Pine City Mall branch is bigger and better than ever. Not only do they showcase their incredible Apple products, accessories and repair services, but their fresh and inventive space has been created for so much more. Enjoy a lounge area to relax and learn about your favourite products, browse through dedicated displays of new products, and benefit from informative discussions, training sessions and other workshops from industry experts. Customers can also enjoy on-site repairs for iOS, battery replacements and iPhone screen repairs. Whatever you may need – be it technical support or more information on your Apple product – you'll find everything you need at the iStore. Situated on the upper-level, opposite H&M clothing.
